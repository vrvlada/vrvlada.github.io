Core Application Capabilities

A state-of-the-art suite designed for modern, high-speed document workflow on Android devices.

Dual OCR Engine

Utilize standard local on-device ML Models & Tesseract 4 (for Cyrillic) for 100% offline private processing. Switch to Google Cloud Vision API for ultra-high accuracy and handwritten script recognition.

Page Flattening (Dewarp)

Integrated OpenCV analytical algorithms automatically flatten warped books, straighten lines, and align margins to output perfectly straight, planar documents.

Universal PDF Converter

Convert over 100+ documents (DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, HTML, TXT, and major images) into standardized PDF documents powered by local layout and Cloudmersive engines.

Digital Signatures (.p12)

Secure and authorize your corporate documents. Cryptographically sign export PDFs using professional personal digital certificates (.p12 keyfiles) and password protection.

PDF Protection & Encryption

Apply robust password policies and industry-standard encryption directly to your documents prior to export, keeping your corporate assets secured.

Searchable PDF Layers

Rather than plain text exports, compile Searchable PDFs (PDF/A). Imprints an invisible coordinates-mapped text layer exactly on top of the original document, allowing easy copy/search.

High-Speed Batch Engine

Process multiple images or pages consecutively. Our batch OCR scanner handles layout pipelines asynchronously, compiling multiple processed pages into a single, cohesive document.

Balanced Token System

Use on-device local tasks completely free and unlimited. Watch short rewarded video advertisements integrated via Google AdMob or buy credit packs to earn tokens for cloud integrations.

How the App Works

A simple, 3-step pipeline converting your raw captures into neat searchable documents.

1

Scan or Import

Take a photo using the guided CameraX screen or select a document/image from your device storage.

2

Enhance & Dewarper

3

Export & Share

Recognized text blocks are reconstructed into standard layout PDF documents, signed, encrypted, or copied instantly.
